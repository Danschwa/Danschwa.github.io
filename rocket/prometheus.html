PROMETHEUS

With over 40 undergraduate team members, Prometheus is the Rocket Project at UCLAâ€™s hybrid rocket competition team. The team, consisting of 100% first year members of the organization (with the exception of the project lead), will be competing in the 2018 Spaceport America Cup as well as the Friends of Amateur Rocketry 10k/30k competitions. Unlike the majority of teams at the competition, our group will be building our very own customized rocket motor system that has provided notably increased performance over the commercial design on which the custom configuration is based.

The goals of this project are lofty, yet focus on education and ensuring that once the competition passes, every member of the team is ready to move onto working on even more complex liquid propulsion systems and vehicles. Whilst cultivating the leadership abilities of new members, the Prometheus team is focused on doing all it can to win the Spaceport America cup and provide its members both education and the pride that comes with achievement.
